Animal Habitats

Homepage Junior Zoologists

Welcome

Animals live in different places around the world. These places are called habitats. A habitat gives an animal everything it needs to survive, such as food, water, shelter, and space.

Let's explore some common animal habitats!

Forest

What is a forest?

A forest is a place with many trees, plants, and animals. Forests can be hot, cool, wet, or dry.

Animals found in forests

  • 🦁 Lion
  • 🐘 Elephant
  • 🐅 Tiger
  • 🦉 Owl
  • 🐒 Monkey
  • 🦌 Deer

Interesting Fact

  • Forests are home to more than half of the world's land plants and animals.

Grassland

What is a grassland?

Grasslands are wide open areas covered mostly with grass. There are very few trees.

Animals found in grasslands

  • 🦓 Zebra
  • 🦒 Giraffe
  • 🦬 Bison
  • 🦌 Antelope
  • 🐇 Rabbit

Interesting Fact

  • Many fast-running animals live in grasslands because there are few places to hide.

Ocean

What is an ocean?

The ocean is a huge body of salt water that covers most of Earth.

Animals found in oceans

  • 🐋 Whale
  • 🐬 Dolphin
  • 🦈 Shark
  • 🐙 Octopus
  • 🐢 Sea Turtle
  • 🦀 Crab

Interesting Fact

  • Oceans cover about 71% of Earth's surface.

Desert

What is a desert?

A desert is a very dry place with very little rainfall. Days are usually hot, and nights can be cold.

Animals found in deserts

  • 🐪 Camel
  • 🦊 Fennec Fox
  • 🐍 Snake
  • 🦎 Lizard
  • 🦂 Scorpion

Interesting Fact

  • Camels can survive for many days without drinking water.

Polar Regions

What are polar regions?

Polar regions are the coldest places on Earth. They are covered with ice and snow for much of the year.

Animals found in polar regions

  • 🐻 Polar Bear
  • 🐧 Penguin
  • 🦭 Seal
  • 🦊 Arctic Fox
  • 🐋 Whale

Interesting Fact

  • Polar bears live in the Arctic, but penguins live in Antarctica.

Mountains

What are mountains

Mountains are very high areas with cool weather and rocky land.

Animals found in mountains

  • 🐐 Mountain Goat
  • 🐆 Snow Leopard
  • 🦅 Eagle
  • 🐻 Brown Bear
  • 🦙 Llama

Interesting Fact

  • Mountain animals have strong legs to climb steep slopes.

Wetlands

What are wetlands?

Wetlands are places where land is covered with shallow water for all or part of the year.

Animals found in wetlands

  • 🐸 Frog
  • 🐊 Crocodile
  • 🦆 Duck
  • 🦩 Flamingo
  • 🐟 Fish

Interesting Fact

  • Wetlands help clean water and provide homes for many animals.

Why is habitats important?

A habitat provides an animal with:

  • Food
  • Water
  • Shelter
  • Space to live and grow

If a habitat is damaged, the animals living there may struggle to survive.
