Welcome

Animals can be divided into two main groups based on whether they have a backbone.

  • Vertebrates
    • Vertebrates have a backbone (spine).
  • Invertebrates
    • Invertebrates do not have a backbone.

Scientists use this feature to classify animals into different groups.

Why Is the Backbone Important?

The backbone:

  • Supports the body.
  • Protects the spinal cord.
  • Helps animals move.

Animals without a backbone often have other ways to protect themselves, such as shells or hard outer coverings.
