Introduction

Animals are living things. They can move, eat, grow, and have babies.

Let's meet some amazing animals!

Lion

  • Name: Lion
  • Type: Wild animal
  • Colour: Golden brown
  • Fun Fact: Lions are called the "King of the Jungle".

Elephant

  • Name: Elephant
  • Type: Wild animal
  • Colour: Grey
  • Fun Fact: Elephants have long trunks.

Cow

  • Name: Cow
  • Type: Farm animal
  • Colour: White, brown, black, or mixed
  • Fun Fact: Cows give us milk.

Dog

  • Name: Dog
  • Type: Pet animal
  • Colour: Many different colours
  • Fun Fact: Dogs are loyal friends.

Duck

  • Name: Duck
  • Type: Farm animal
  • Colour: White, brown, or colourful
  • Fun Fact: Ducks can swim and walk.

Owl

  • Name: Owl
  • Type: Wild bird
  • Colour: Brown, grey, or white
  • Fun Fact: Owls are awake at night.

Remember

  • Some animals live with people.
  • Some animals live in forests.
  • Some animals can fly.
  • Some animals love water.
